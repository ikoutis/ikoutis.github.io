Yiannis Koutis Machine Learning Notes
A course in 20 modules

Machine Learning — understand why,
not just how.

Most resources tell you what an algorithm does. These notes focus on why — what each algorithm assumes about the data, and why those assumptions are reasonable, useful, or risky. Written to be read in order, from k-NN through recurrent networks.

Yiannis Koutis
Associate Professor, NJIT
